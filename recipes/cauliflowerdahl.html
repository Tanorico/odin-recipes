Cauliflower Dahl

Description

This healthy dhal is full of aromatic spice and healthy legumes. Serve it with yoghurt and naan bread for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons medium hot Indian curry paste
  • 1 cup (200g) dried red lentils
  • 3 cups Campbell's Real Stock Vegetable
  • 1 green chilli
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 500g cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 teaspoons turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes
  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, garlic and curry paste, then cook, stirring, for five minutes or until onion softens.
  3. Add lentils and stir well to coat.
  4. Add stock and tomato paste, bring to the boil, then cover and simmer over low heat for 15 minutes.
  5. Return heat to medium, add cauliflower and simmer, covered, for 8-10 minutes until vegetables are tender.
